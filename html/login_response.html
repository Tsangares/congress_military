Third Party Senators in Congress – January 12, 2023

Over the country’s history, there have been more than seventy senators who represented third parties or did not identify with any party. In this Congress Report, former congressional reporter, Deborah Kalb, discusses the impact of past independent and third party senators in Congress, and probes into how the 118th Congress will be affected by Senator Kyrsten Sinema’s decision to become independent.
